Corporate Finance

Financing is a very important decision for the business. From start up to working capital, and from day to day cashflow to investment, the financing option plays a major part.

Raising of finance depends on the company strategy. Short term finance (such as. bank loans, overdrafts) may be suitable for business but on the other hand, bonds and shares may be an option for long term finance.

Our Corporate Finance servies include:

  • Examining your business finance structure

  • Identifying your finance need whether it is short term or long term

  • Categorising the source of finance

  • Evaluating the cost of capital and reporting on finance strategies.

  • Advice on taking finance
