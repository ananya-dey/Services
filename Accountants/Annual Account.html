Annual Account

Every business regardless of registration, be it Sole Trader, Partnership or Limited company, needs to prepare their accounts annually or according to their year end as per the UK statutory requirements and tax regulations. All of our accountants prepare a set of accounts as we do, but our approach involves value added services with competitive fees.

Within our services we take care of all the book-keeping related to your business. Our dedicated team provides free consultation on keeping records and process invoices. We also advise on how to operate accounting transaction correctly, how to run your business effectively, and methods of improving your business operations. We also provide period end accounts to state the performance of your business within specific timeframes.

Our unique service includes account preparation for standalone self-employed businesses (eg, minicab, hairdresser, carpenter, painter etc). We also offer you self-assessment tax return advice with basic book-keeping and account preparation principals.

The account format depends on the company requirement or type of the company. From your business records we will prepare the following as per requisite:

•     Profit and Loss Account
•    Income and Expenditure Account
•    Balance Sheet / Statement of Financial Position
•    Fixed Asset Schedule

We aim to complete annual accounts after your end and ahead of schedule to avoid any risk of penalty. We also resolve or identify any potential problems before the year end. Why don’t you give us a call or email for further consultation?
