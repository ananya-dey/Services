Forensic Accountancy

Not only the big businesses need the forensic accountancy services, small businesses may need them too. Many cash trading business such as a grocery store or fast food outlet may identify loosing of money or theft of stock. In many cases, it is not always possible to identify which is being stolen or missing.

Analysis of your situation and need for a forensic approach can be undertaken based on your records. We will analyse your stock and financial ratios to identify the discrepancies. Different businesses would need different techniques of forensic accountancy. So it is always better to prepare for the worst and get have your operations organized and supervised.
